Understanding Your Mortgage
Your monthly payment consists of:
  • Principal & Interest (P&I) - pays down your loan
  • Escrow - covers property tax and insurance
  • PMI may be required if down payment is less than 20%
Typical range: $100,000 - $1,000,000
Need help determining price?
Consider these factors:
  • Local market conditions
  • Recent comparable sales
  • Property condition and features
  • Location and neighborhood
20% of purchase price
About down payments
Common down payment options:
  • 20% - Traditional, no PMI required
  • 3.5% - FHA minimum
  • 3% - Some conventional loans
  • 0% - VA or USDA if you qualify
Current market rates: 6.5% - 7.5%
What affects your rate?
Factors affecting your rate:
  • Credit score (higher score = lower rate)
  • Down payment amount
  • Loan term
  • Loan type (Conventional, FHA, VA)
  • Property type and use
Common terms: 15, 20, or 30 years
Choosing your term
Term comparison:
  • 30 year - Lower monthly payments, more interest
  • 15 year - Higher payments, less total interest
  • 20 year - Balance between payment and interest
